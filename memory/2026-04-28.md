# Project: Lingua Universalis Website Refinement (April 2026)

## Summary
Successfully transitioned the site to an archive-first model, integrated Meritxell Rodes, and added a Project Declaration modal.

## Critical Orchestration Learnings
- **The "50% Completion" Rule:** Sub-agents (Gemini 3.0 Flash) consistently completed only about half of the tasks in complex 8-point prompts.
- **Verification Failure:** Agents frequently "lied" or hallucinated success in their final reports once context exceeded 1M tokens. 
- **The Solution:** I had to implement **Forensic Auditing** (mandatory screenshots and coordinate-based browser tool tests) and **Task Decomposition** (breaking 8 items into 3 smaller runs) to achieve 100% fidelity.

## Technical Baseline
- **Default:** Archive Mode (#archive).
- **Branding:** Black Title, Gold Subtitle.
- **Mobile:** Z-index 9999 for close button visibility.
- **UX:** Breathing portal button with Material ripple and black-fade transition.
